How work moves

One request. The right people. A clear outcome.

Whether it starts as an email, file, customer question or internal task, the work stays connected from arrival to completion.

01

Work comes in

Bring in an email, document, request or customer conversation. Buinee keeps the source and context together.

02

Someone takes ownership

Assign it, discuss it, add instructions and complete the next step without losing decisions across separate tools.

03

The outcome is recorded

Send the reply, approve the document, close the task or issue the final file with a history everyone can trust.

Roles & visibility

Give everyone the access their work requires.

Buinee supports different responsibilities without forcing every business into the same job titles. People see their own work, shared team work, or the full workspace according to their role.

Tier 3

Team member

Handles day-to-day work.

  • Manages assigned work
  • Works with messages and files
  • Requests review when needed

Sees their own work only.

Tier 2

Team lead

Coordinates people and decisions.

  • Reviews team work
  • Approves or returns requests
  • Keeps work moving

Sees their own work and their team's.

Tier 1

Supervisor

Runs the workspace.

  • Oversees teams and workflows
  • Sets access and business rules
  • Adds and removes people

Sees the whole workspace.

Inside your dashboard

Every person gets an assistant that understands their work.

Once your company is in, each person's dashboard comes with an AI assistant — one that works with the actual context: mailbox conversations, documents, customer records, team instructions and outstanding tasks. Not a generic chatbot bolted to the corner of the screen.

Your inbox

It reads the mail before you do

Connect your work mailbox and it goes through overnight — sorting what came in, drafting the replies that are obvious, and putting the things that genuinely need you at the top.

  • Sorts requests, updates, questions and noise
  • Drafts replies in your own tone
  • Flags risks, changes and unanswered messages

Nothing is ever sent without you pressing send.

Your documents

Hand it a file and ask

Give it a report, spreadsheet, contract, proposal, invoice or internal guide. It reads the document itself, finds the useful details, and helps you act on what is missing or important.

  • PDFs, scans, Word and Excel
  • Summarises, compares and extracts details
  • Explains what needs attention and why

Built for dependable work

AI can assist. Your rules and records remain in control.

Business software earns its place by being predictable. Buinee separates suggestions from facts, keeps actions reviewable, and records important changes.

Facts stay verifiable

Stored records, calculated figures and completed actions remain distinct from AI suggestions, so people can see what is known and what is proposed.

People approve important actions

Drafts, summaries and recommendations stay ready for review. Sending, approving and changing records remains a deliberate human action.

Your company's data is yours

Every record belongs to one company and is scoped to it. No document, figure or conversation is visible outside the organisation that created it.
