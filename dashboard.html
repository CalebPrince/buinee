How roles work

Visibility and approval responsibility
Preparer

Creates vouchers and sees only their own work. Cannot approve or reject vouchers.

Approver

Sees their own work and Preparers’ work. Can review submitted vouchers, but never their own.

Supervisor

Sees the whole company, manages join requests, and controls company-wide AI settings.
